On clinical evidence, the cardiovascular outcomes trials are the strongest data we have: LEADER, SUSTAIN-6, and SELECT collectively document significant reductions in major adverse cardiovascular events across diabetic and non-diabetic populations. We would need cleaner head-to-head trial data to separate those contributors.
